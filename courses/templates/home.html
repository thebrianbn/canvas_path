Welcome to CanvasPath


CanvasPath is intended to be used as a course management system for students and professors at the Lion State University. Start by clicking the Login button on the top right to log in to your Lion State University account. Users may then view their University profile information and update their password through the Profile page. All necessary information to be viewed by students and professors regarding courses can be seen in the Dashboard.

Students will see each course that they are enrolled in on their Dashboard. Once they click on a course, all relevant course information will be shared, such as course details, professor contact information, and grades for each homework and exam. Also included is summary statistics for each assignment.

Professors will see each course that they are teaching on their Dashboard. Once they click on a course, course details will be shown, their course roster, and all assignments in the course. They will see summary statistics for each of their assignments, and have the option to submit grades for those assignments. If they choose to submit grades, they will be shown a table of each student and their grade. Each grade entry must be filled out in order to save results. At the very bottom, of the course page, they have options to create homework and exam assignments. If clicked on, they will be taken to the assignment creation page, where they have to enter the wanted assignment number and details.

Administrators may login to the system with the path "/admin/", email admin@lionstate.edu, and password test12345. Once logged in, they will be able to view all database relations and instances. They have the ability to create, update, or delete any instances. This allow admins to assign professors to courses, students to enrollments, and so on.
